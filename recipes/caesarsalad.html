Caesar Salad

Description

Caesar salad is a great easy dish. Eat it, don't eat it, I don't care.

Ingredients

  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Caesar Dressing
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Croutons

Directions

  1. Chop up lettuce into bite size pieces
  2. Add croutons
  3. Add dressing
  4. Mix together with parmesan cheese and serve.
